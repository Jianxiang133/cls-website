We welcome applications from families who share our vision for an education rooted in faith, inquiry, and character.

2026–27 Cycle

How to Apply

Three intakes are open across the academic year. We assess each application individually — academic record, character references, and a family conversation.

S1

Secondary 1 Entry

Through the Hong Kong Secondary School Places Allocation system, plus our Direct Subsidy interview round in November.

S2–S4

Interim Admission

Limited mid-year places open in April for transferring students. Online registration with academic records and a portfolio of activities.

S5–S6

Senior Transfer

Considered case-by-case, subject to elective compatibility and HKDSE planning.

Process

The Four Steps

1

Enquire

Email info@clsedu.hk or fill in the form below.

2

Visit

Join an Open Day or book a one-to-one campus tour.

3

Apply

Submit academic records, references, and a family statement.

4

Interview

A conversation with the student and family. Decision within four weeks.
