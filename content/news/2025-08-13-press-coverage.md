Local press recently profiled Cognitio's approach to weaving Chinese cultural heritage into our STEM and innovation programmes.

The piece featured our Wisdom Valley field studies, the bilingual humanities pathway, and a student-led project documenting Sai Kung's traditional fishing villages.

"We don't see heritage and innovation as opposites," said Principal Tang in the interview. "They're the same impulse — to look carefully at the world and make something useful from what you see."
