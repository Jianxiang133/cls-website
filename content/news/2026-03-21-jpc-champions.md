Our Junior Police Call (JPC) team took home the top territory-wide title at this year's competition, with judges noting the team's calm decision-making in the crisis simulation round.

Senior JPC officer Form 5's Andy Lee credited the win to "two years of weekly drills and a lot of patient mentoring from our advisors."
