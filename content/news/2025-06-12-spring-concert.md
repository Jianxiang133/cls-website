The annual Spring Concert played to a full house at the school auditorium, with the orchestra and choir performing a programme that ranged from Bach to a new commission by alumna composer Cherry Yip.

All ticket proceeds support our Sai Kung outreach partners, including the after-school programme at Pak Tam Chung village.

A recording of the concert will be made available to families through the parent portal later this term.
