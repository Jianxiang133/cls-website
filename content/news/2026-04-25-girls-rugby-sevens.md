Our senior girls' rugby side completed an unbeaten run at this year's Inter-School Sevens, lifting the trophy in front of a home crowd at Wisdom Valley.

Coach Mr Chan praised the squad's discipline at the breakdown and their willingness to play for each other. "It was the best team performance I've seen from a Cognitio side," he said.

Captain Megan Wong added that the win was "for everyone who came before us, and everyone who will come after."
