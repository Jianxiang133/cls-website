S4 students completed a five-day marine biology field camp at Hoi Ha Wan Marine Park, surveying coral cover and water quality alongside marine ecologists from a local university.

Each pair of students contributed a stretch of transect data to an ongoing citizen-science programme, and produced a short field report on a species of their choice.

The camp is part of our Wisdom Valley Outdoor Learning Programme, which has run uninterrupted since 1995.
