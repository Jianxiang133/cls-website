Outstanding Teaching Award for Values Education

29 Aug 2025

Two members of the Cognitio teaching staff received the Outstanding Teaching Award for Values Education at this year's territory-wide ceremony.

Both teachers were recognised for sustained, classroom-level work — not for one-off projects, but for the way values are woven into how subjects are taught day to day.

The school congratulates them on the recognition and thanks the panel for visiting Wisdom Valley.
