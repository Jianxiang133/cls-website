The Curriculum

A continuous six-year journey from S1 to S6 — disciplined, bilingual, and deliberately broad.

Structure

From Foundation to Pathway

Junior Secondary builds breadth. Senior Secondary builds depth. The thread that runs through both is inquiry — the habit of asking good questions and following them honestly.

S1–S3

Junior Secondary

Chinese, English, mathematics, integrated science, humanities, IT, visual arts, music, PE, and Christian Education. Outdoor learning is integrated, not extra-curricular.

S4–S6

Senior Secondary (HKDSE)

Four core subjects plus two to three electives chosen from sciences, humanities, business, and visual arts. Citizenship & Social Development is taught alongside our values curriculum.

+

STEM & Innovation

Maker studio, robotics squad, and university research partnerships. Award-winning entries at international invention exhibitions.

⌘

Languages & Humanities

Bilingual fluency in Chinese and English, with a humanities pathway in history, geography, philosophy, and literature.
